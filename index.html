Financial Econometrics — interactive lab

Play with the models, quiz yourself, ask the tutor. Built to go with the lectures and tutorials.

These tools turn the trickiest parts of the module into something you can move with your hands. Drag a parameter, watch the autocorrelation change; push a forecast out and see the interval fan. Everything runs in your browser, with no login and nothing collected. Best used right before or after the matching tutorial.
